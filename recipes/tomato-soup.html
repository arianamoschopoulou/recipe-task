Tomato and Red Pepper Soup

This is the best soup for a cold winter's night - best served with a fresh slice of sourdough bread

Ingredients

  • 500gr Cherry tomatoes
  • 2 Red peppers
  • 1 Red onion
  • 2-3 Garlic cloves
  • 2-3 tbsp Olive oil
  • Freh basil or 1 tsp of pesto
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Method

  1. Turn the oven to 160*C
  2. Chop the cherry tomatoes, red peppers the onion and garlic and place on a baking tray
  3. Add olive oil, pinch of salt & pepper and pop into the oven for 20 mins
  4. Remove from oven when well roasted and add to blender with the basil or pesto
  5. Blend until smooth
  6. Add salt and pepper to taste

TOP TIP: sieve the soup for a smooth velvety finish
