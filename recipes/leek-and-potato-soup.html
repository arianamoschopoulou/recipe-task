Leek and Potato Soup

Another classic dish, perfect served with warm toasted bread

Ingredients

  • 2 large leeks
  • 1 medium - large potato
  • 500 ml of vegetable stock
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Method

  1. Chop the leeks and put them into a pot with the olive oil and fry gently until soft
  2. Chop the potatoes into small cubes and add to the pot with the leeks
  3. Add the stock and boil for 30 minutes on medium/low heat
  4. When all the get is soft, add salt and pepper to taste and blend until smooth
  5. Serve fresh!
